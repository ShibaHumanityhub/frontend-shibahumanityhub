Questions That Matter

How are the Star Souls funded between visits?

Continuous readiness comes from Mercy Circle holdings. Training, vests, transport, and basic guardian support. Actual visits and guardian salary portions are released when sponsorships or higher circles activate.

Do the dogs live with guardians full time?

Yes. They are family members who also do important work. The Pay It Forward Adoption Chain model ensures the guardian is supported so the dog can focus on healing others.

How do we know the visits are real?

Guardians provide regular updates, photos, and stories. These will be logged in the soulbound passports. Transparency is non-negotiable.
