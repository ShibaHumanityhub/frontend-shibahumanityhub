How the Unified Rescue Registry Works

The Moment a Dog is Rescued

A soulbound passport is minted on the blockchain. The rescue story, intake photos, and first health check are recorded forever.

Every Step of the Journey

Vet visits, training milestones, foster homes, and personality notes are added. The passport grows with the dog.

Forever Home

When adopted, the passport moves with the dog to the new family. Their story together is added. The bond is now on-chain.

When the funding comes through, every single rescue dog will finally have their whole beautiful journey honored and remembered forever. No more stories lost to time.
