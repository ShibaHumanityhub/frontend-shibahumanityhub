The Matching Process
How it works
  • • Seniors 60+ complete a short, honest form

Wellness Check Ins and a Real Safety Net

Trusted people from the program check in gently two to four times a year. Video or in person. The senior just shows that the dog is happy and healthy. Everything stays private. If something ever needs attention we pause the support only long enough to help. The dog stays right where it is.
